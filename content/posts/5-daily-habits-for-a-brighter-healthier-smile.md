Welcome back to the Harbor Smile blog! While we love seeing you in the office for your regular checkups, the truth is that a healthy smile is built at home. What you do every day plays the biggest role in preventing cavities, keeping your gums healthy, and maintaining fresh breath.

If you want to keep your teeth looking and feeling their best, here are five simple daily habits to incorporate into your routine:

1. Brush Twice, But Don't Rush
   We all know we should brush our teeth morning and night, but technique matters just as much as frequency. Make sure you are brushing for a full two minutes each time. Use a soft-bristled brush and gentle, circular motions to clean your teeth without damaging your enamel or irritating your gums.
2. Floss Like You Mean It
   Brushing only cleans about 60% of your tooth surfaces. The tight spaces between your teeth are breeding grounds for plaque and bacteria. Flossing at least once a day removes trapped food particles and helps prevent gum disease. If traditional string floss is difficult for you, try using floss picks or a water flosser!
3. Hydrate with Water
   Water is your mouth's best friend. Drinking plenty of water throughout the day helps wash away lingering food particles and dilutes the acids produced by bacteria in your mouth. Plus, staying hydrated promotes healthy saliva production, which is your body's natural defense against tooth decay.
4. Be Mindful of Sugary and Acidic Snacks
   Every time you eat sugary or highly acidic foods, your enamel is temporarily weakened. Try to limit frequent snacking on sweets, sodas, and citrus fruits. If you do indulge, try to rinse your mouth out with water afterward to help neutralize the acids before you brush.
5. Don't Skip Your Checkups
   Even the best at-home routine needs professional support! Regular cleanings and exams allow our team to remove hardened tartar that brushing can't tackle. It also gives us the chance to catch any potential issues early, saving you time and discomfort in the long run.
